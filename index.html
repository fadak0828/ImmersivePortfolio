About

Kai Yu is an artist and researcher whose work explores the intersections of digital technology, human perception, and the natural world. Through large-scale installations and virtual environments, Yu crafts sensorial experiences that challenge our understanding of reality and presence.

Utilizing volumetric light, generative algorithms, and spatial sound, Yu's practice is rooted in a philosophy of "digital warmth." The work aims to evoke a sense of calm and connection, transforming sterile digital tools into conduits for profound, museum-like contemplation.
